TripTO

Visakhapatnam

OVER THE WEEKEND

Start your journey from divis bridge watch the morning sunrise.

Move to Bheemli beach and watch the beautiful scluptors and the calming beach with a spicy tangy corn

After enjoying the local breakfast move to “Erramatti Dibalu” spend some time taking pictures

Then move to Thotlakonda and witness natures best view from top of the hill and also a beach with natural arch

Its finally time for l unch and some fun in the shore, enjoy the best sea food in vizag and take part in the water sports for some time in the Rushikonda

When its the end of afternoon we move to Kailasagiri , experience the rope way to see the top view of vizag and then spend some time in kailasagiri

The show stopper RK beach is the final destination , watch the historic crafts like submarine and fighter plane and have some local delicacies

If time permits Dolphin nose would be covered when there is a light house
